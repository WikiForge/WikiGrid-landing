Frequently asked questions

If you can’t find what you’re looking for, email our support team and we’ll help you get started with WikiGrid.

    • Does WikiGrid support VisualEditor?

      Yes! WikiGrid offers VisualEditor for easy, intuitive editing — no wikitext required.

    • Is WikiGrid really free?

      Yes! WikiGrid is free for all communities. No hidden fees, no credit card required.

    • Can I migrate my existing wiki?

      Yes, you can import your existing MediaWiki site to WikiGrid. Contact support for help with migration.

    • Is WikiGrid secure?

      WikiGrid uses secure authentication and regular backups to keep your data safe.

    • Can I use custom domains?

      Yes! WikiGrid supports custom domains for your wiki. Set it up easily in ManageWiki.

    • What is ManageWiki?

      ManageWiki is a powerful tool included with WikiGrid that lets you configure your wiki’s settings, extensions, and appearance with ease.

    • How do I manage users?

      WikiGrid provides centralized account management. Easily add, remove, and assign roles to users from your dashboard.

    • Will you add more features?

      We’re always working to improve WikiGrid. Suggest new features and we’ll do our best to add them!

    • I lost my password, how do I get into my account?

      Use the password reset feature on the login page, or contact support for help.
